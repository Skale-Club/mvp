<objective>
Wire the two client-side conversion events that the marketing dashboard depends on: phone-click and booking-started.

Purpose: CONV-02 (phone clicked) and CONV-04 (booking started) — covered server-side in Phase 4 — need their client triggers wired so `attribution_conversions` rows accumulate. Without these, the Conversions tab in Phase 6 shows only `lead_created` rows.

Approach (from CONTEXT.md D-17/D-19):
- Use the utility helper `fireConversionEvent` from `@/lib/attribution` (no hook call, no prop drilling). The utility reads `mvp_vid` directly from localStorage, so any component anywhere in the tree can fire conversions safely.
- "Booking started" on this site === clicking any CTA that opens the LeadFormModal. There is no separate cart/checkout flow; the lead form IS the booking funnel entry point. All five `setIsFormOpen(true)` callsites are the booking-start triggers.

Output:
- 5 modified files, each adding a single `fireConversionEvent` call alongside the existing click handler. No new files. No layout/UI changes. No new state.
</objective>
